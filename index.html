How Pixel Streaming Works

The experience runs on a powerful cloud computer, not on the buyer’s device. The real-time 3D home is rendered remotely, then streamed to the browser as a smooth interactive video feed.

When someone taps a finish, opens a floor plan, changes a colour, or moves through the space, their input is sent back to the cloud instantly. The cloud updates the scene and streams the new view back in real time.

This means customers can explore a high-end visualizer on a phone, tablet, laptop, or showroom screen without installing software or needing expensive hardware. They simply open a link and interact with the home.

Because the demanding 3D rendering stays in the cloud, the visualizer can be updated centrally as a home model, finish, floor plan, or feature changes. Buyers and sales teams always open the current experience from the same simple link.

Unlike a pre-rendered video, Pixel Streaming remains fully interactive. Every choice is calculated and shown live, so a customer can test ideas, compare options, and immediately understand how their selections affect the home.

It also makes the experience practical for showhomes, sales centres, remote appointments, and early marketing—giving people a polished, high-end walkthrough without asking them to download or maintain specialist software.
